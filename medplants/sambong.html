Sambong

Sambong (Blumea balsamifera) – A tropical medicinal plant known for its broad, hairy leaves and distinct camphor-like aroma. Sambong is widely used in traditional Filipino medicine for its diuretic properties, making it effective in treating kidney stones and urinary tract infections. It is also commonly used to alleviate coughs, colds, and high blood pressure. The leaves are often brewed into tea or applied topically as a poultice for various ailments. Sambong grows well in warm, tropical environments and is a staple in herbal gardens across the Philippines.
